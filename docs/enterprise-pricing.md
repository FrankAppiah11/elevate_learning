## Pricing Philosophy

Elevate uses a transparent, tiered pricing model designed for institutional budgets. Pricing consists of two components:

1. **One-time implementation fee** — Covers platform configuration, LMS integration, instructor training, and pilot support
2. **Annual license fee** — Covers the platform subscription, AI processing (OpenAI), cloud infrastructure (Vercel + Supabase), ongoing updates, and support

There are no per-student transaction fees, no hidden costs, and no charges for feature updates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can we start with a pilot before committing to a full license?**
A: Yes. We recommend a 6-week pilot with 2–3 courses. The Starter tier is designed for this. Pilot fees apply toward the annual license if you proceed.

**Q: Who owns the student data?**
A: Your institution. All data is stored in your institution's Supabase database. We do not share, sell, or train AI models on institutional data.

**Q: Does this replace our LMS?**
A: No. Elevate integrates with your existing LMS via LTI 1.3. Students launch Elevate from within Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, or Brightspace.

**Q: What AI model powers the tutors?**
A: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini. The AI is accessed via API — no student data is used for model training per OpenAI's API terms.

**Q: Are there multi-year discounts?**
A: Yes. 2-year contracts receive a 10% discount on the annual license. 3-year contracts receive 15%.

**Q: Can we customize the grading weights?**
A: Enterprise tier includes custom rubric weighting. Starter and Professional use the standard 60/30/10 scheme.
